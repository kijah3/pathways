About Pathways

Who we are, what we believe, and how we serve young Zambians.

Who We Are

Pathways is an initiative under RISE Ubuntu — providing education, mentorship, and community development programmes to equip young Zambians for life and leadership.

Pathways was created to address a fundamental gap: young people in Zambia who are capable and motivated but lack access to structured learning, mentorship, and opportunity. Operating under RISE Ubuntu, Pathways delivers two core programme streams — Education Pathways and Community Pathways — reaching students across five districts. Every programme is designed around the belief that young people, given the right environment and support, will build a stronger Zambia.

  • Structured academic and vocational education
  • One-on-one and group mentorship
  • Community enrichment and enterprise support
  • Employment readiness and employer linkages

Our Vision & Mission

The principles that guide everything we do.

Our Vision

A Zambia where every young person — regardless of background — has access to the knowledge, skills, and networks needed to lead a purposeful, productive life and contribute meaningfully to their community and nation.

Our Mission

“To develop students into responsible, capable leaders.”

“To provide structured education, mentorship, and community engagement opportunities for youth.”

Our Governance

Pathways operates under the oversight of RISE Ubuntu's board and a dedicated team of programme leaders committed to accountability, transparency, and impact.

Board Oversight

Pathways is governed by the RISE Ubuntu board of directors, which provides strategic direction, fiduciary oversight, and ensures all programmes align with the organisation's mission and values. The board includes representatives from education, civil society, and the private sector.

About RISE Ubuntu

RISE Ubuntu is a registered non-profit organisation based in Zambia, dedicated to the holistic development of young people and communities. "Ubuntu" — the African philosophy of shared humanity — sits at the heart of everything the organisation does.

Through Pathways and other initiatives, RISE Ubuntu invests in education, leadership, and social enterprise as pathways out of poverty and into purpose. The organisation works in partnership with government, civil society, and the private sector to maximise reach and impact.
