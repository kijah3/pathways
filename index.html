Our Programs

Pathways offers two core streams — Education Pathways and Community Pathways — each designed to meet young people where they are and take them further.

Education Pathways

FPP

Foundational Pathways Programme

A structured academic foundation programme equipping students with essential literacy, numeracy, and life skills needed to thrive in higher education and the workforce.

APP

Advanced Pathways Programme

An advanced curriculum for students who have completed the foundational level, deepening critical thinking, leadership, and vocational competencies.

Community Pathways

Children Enrichment

After-school programmes providing safe, nurturing spaces for children to learn and grow.

Employment

Job readiness training, CV workshops, and employer linkages for youth entering the workforce.

Partnerships

Collaborating with NGOs, government bodies, and businesses to expand community impact.

Small-Scale Enterprises

Mentorship and seed support for young entrepreneurs launching community-based businesses.
