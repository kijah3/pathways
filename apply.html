Start Your Pathways Journey

Applications are open for the Future Pathways Program and Access Pathways Program. Join a community of exceptional young Zambians.

Choose Your Programme

Read the eligibility criteria carefully before applying. You may only apply to one programme per intake.

FPP Flagship Programme

Future Pathways Program

Highly selective 18-month residential programme for top-performing secondary school graduates — focused on higher education, personal formation, and leadership.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed Grade 12 (secondary school graduate)
  • Demonstrated academic excellence
  • Zambian national or resident
  • Willing to commit to the residential phase (Year 1)
  • Strong motivation for higher education or leadership
APP Access Programme

Access Pathways Program

A one-year programme for high-potential students providing mentorship, work experience, and university guidance — ensuring no student is left behind.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Completed Grade 12
  • Not selected for FPP intake
  • Motivated and coachable
  • Zambian national or resident
  • Willing to engage consistently over 12 months

Application Timeline

What to expect after you apply.

1

Applications Open

October – November

Submit your application form online or drop it off in person at a Pathways office.

2

Shortlisting

December

The admissions team reviews all applications and prepares a shortlist.

3

Interviews

December – January

Shortlisted candidates are contacted and invited to interview.

4

Selection & Offers

January

Successful applicants are notified and offered a place in their programme.

5

Programme Begins

February

FPP residential phase begins. APP orientation and induction starts.
